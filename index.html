Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Who we are

LicenceReady (licenceready.org) is a UK driving theory test preparation service. If you have any questions about this policy you can contact us at licencereadyuk@gmail.com.

Information we collect

When you create an account we collect your email address. When you make a purchase, payment is processed securely by Stripe and we do not store your card details. We also store your quiz progress and test scores so you can track your improvement.

How we use your information

We use your information solely to provide the LicenceReady service: managing your account, processing payments and saving your progress. We do not sell your data to third parties.

AI Tutor feature

Questions you ask the AI Tutor are sent securely to our AI provider to generate a response. We do not use your questions for any purpose other than answering them.

Third party services

We use Stripe for payment processing and Supabase for secure account storage. Both providers have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data.

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing licencereadyuk@gmail.com.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy please contact us at licencereadyuk@gmail.com.
